# Geek Haven - User Manual

## Welcome
Geek Haven is your personal sanctuary for books. It organizes your local PDFs, EPUBs, and Audiobooks while offering intelligent features to enhance your reading experience.

## Features

### 1. The Library (Book Hub)
- **Scanning**: Click the **+** button in the Library to scan folders on your device.
- **Grouping**: The app automatically groups file formats (PDF, EPUB, Audio) into a single "Book" entry.
- **Details**: Tap any book to enter the **Book Hub**, where you can choose how to read or listen.

### 2. Smart Reading & Listening
- **PDF Reader**: 
    - **Zoom**: Pinch to zoom in on details.
    - **Read Mode**: Tap the brightness icon to switch between Day, Sepia, and Night modes.
    - **Auto-Flip**: Tap the Play button to automatically turn pages (Hands-free).
    - Your page is *automatically saved*.
- **Audio Player**: 
    - **Controls**: Seekbar, Speed Control, and Skip buttons.
    - **Sleep Timer**: Set a timer to stop playback automatically.
    - **Lock Mode**: Tap the Lock icon to prevent accidental touches.
    - Playback position is *automatically saved*.
- **Memory Anchor**: When you stop reading, a popup asks "Leaving so soon?". Use this to type a quick note (e.g., "Stopped at the cliffhanger"). This note appears at the top of the Book Hub when you return.

### 3. Intelligence
- **Reading DNA**: Go to **Settings** to see your stats (Total Books, Hours Listened, etc.).
- **Recommendations**: The **Home Screen** suggests unread books from your library.
- **Search**: Use the **Discover Books** bar on the Home Screen to find books in your library or search Google Books for new titles.

## Tips
- **Nature Mode**: The interface is designed to be calm and distraction-free.
- **Privacy**: All data stays on your device (except for Google Books searches).

Enjoy your haven!
